Research Interests

My research centers on Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), where I strive to understand and address users' evolving needs, designing innovative solutions that enhance their daily interactions with technology. By focusing on practical, impactful advancements in human-computer interactivity, my work aims to improve how people engage with digital systems across diverse platforms. Key areas of interest include:

  • Input Devices and Interaction Techniques
  • Ubiquitous Analytics
  • Information Visualization
  • Mobile and Wearable User Interfaces
