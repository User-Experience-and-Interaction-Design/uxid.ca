Welcome to the User Experience and Interaction Design Lab

Dr. Ali Neshati leads the UXID Lab at Ontario Tech University, a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) research hub dedicated to advancing user experience design and information visualization. As an Assistant Professor, Dr. Neshati focuses on improving how people interact with digital systems, exploring cutting-edge technologies like wearable devices, small-screen interactions, and ubiquitous computing. Prior to this, he completed a prestigious Postdoctoral Fellowship at the University of Waterloo, working on innovative HCI projects under the mentorship of renowned experts Dr. Daniel Vogel and Dr. Jian Zhao. His Ph.D. research at the University of Manitoba specialized in optimizing data visualization for smartwatch displays, while his earlier work during his M.Sc. at Sharif University of Technology involved complex studies on graph theory and Latin squares. Dr. Neshati’s work in HCI is aimed at shaping the future of user interaction across diverse platforms and devices.
